LLaMA from Scratch ↗
Transformer · Autoregressive LM · RoPE + RMSNorm + SwiGLU

Full implementation of the LLaMA architecture with no high-level abstractions. The goal was to understand exactly how modern LLMs differ from vanilla transformers: rotary positional encoding replaces learned embeddings, RMS normalization replaces LayerNorm, and SwiGLU replaces ReLU in the feed-forward network. Each of these choices has measurable effects on training stability and parameter efficiency, which I explored through ablations at small scale.

SmallGPT from Scratch ↗
GPT-style · Decoder-only · Minimal Transformer

A deliberately minimal GPT built to study how small decoder-only transformers behave under different parameter budgets. I experimented with context window size, embedding dimension, and number of heads to understand the interplay between capacity and overfitting. The training loop is written from scratch with a character-level tokenizer and manual gradient accumulation, so loss dynamics are fully observable.
